About Manage.me

About The App

Manage.me is a state-of-art time management product which focuses on transforming the way you make your schedules and manage your time and tasks. We recoment using the app in compliance with S.M.A.R.T principles which we are inspired by. Our product aims at helping you to organise your tasks and allocate them in a more efficient way by providing a an agenda -view of all your allocated tasks and free time so that you can solve the puzzle of your schedule in a faster and easier intutive way. To know more about how our app works please visit these Help Docs to learn more.

Analytic weekly Agenda View

The main feature of the app is its Analytic Agenda View , this offers a weekly view of ones allocated task in a form simiar to a vertical gantt chart. this gives a clear insight to the user of his tasks , allocated time and available time.

One Click Easy Task allocation and Modification

We believe in simplicity and productivity , hence our product offers a minimalistic user interface and quick shortcuts which allow a user to add tasks just by clicks and modify them with drag and drop making it extremely easy and fast to make schedules on it.

Deep Customizability

We belive in freedom of the user and respect user's will, Unlike others we dont restruct users to limited customization options like only a set of colors to choose from for events , we offer deep cutomization. Event detail page can be opened by a click on th events which offers hex code level colour options allowing user to have any colour he wants for any of his events.

What is S.M.A.R.T ?

S.M.A.R.T is a theory developed by the famous physcologist Edwin Locke in 1981. the abreveation S.M.A.R.T refres to an anlysis technique for goal setting as follows :-
Specific : what exactly will i be doing ? this should be clear while setting the Goal.
Measurable : there must be a min ammount of work decided to be finished , for example , in studies how will a person know that he has done the task properly - read , test , make notes.
Acceptable : One should know that is he pereapered to start working in that moment of time , a person should be in the suitable state to do the work. Half heaarted efforts never go fruitful.
Realistic : One has to set achievable realistic targets , setting unachivable gials give a psedo sense of faliure and results in demotivation.
Time bound : The goal should not be only target based , one must have an appropriate time limit on the task as well.
